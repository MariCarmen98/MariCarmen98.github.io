ANGLE

THE MULTI-TASK SPEAKER

ANGLE is a small size speaker that allows you to take it with you anywhere thanks to its 16cm side. It also has a space to support mobile and free your hands when watching any video or presentation. Lastly, it is worth nothing that its handle fulfills a double function: In addition to being a handle, it can be used as a jack, to connect the mobile device to the speaker although it is not available with bluetooth.

It has been created using basic shapes, such as the line and the triangle, to achieve the greatest simplicity possible. After considering several assembly options, we decided to prioritize space optimization and minimize its dimensions to facilitate transportation.

1.Conceptualisation phase: Once the scope defined that we had to create a speaker, we worked on different brainstorming sessions and creative methods. We defined the criteria that seemed most appropriate to us, deciding that we wanted to maintain simplicity.

2.First 3D modeling: Once we had decided on the path to follow, in this case, continuing with a triangle, we took measurements of the components to be introduced and created the first 3D model.

3.Reorganization & final 3D: This phase includes resizing and optimization after confirming that in the first model, the parts did not fit correctly.
